Q&A
What can I do in the library?
keyboard_arrow_down
Every one is allowed to explore published papers in the digital library. After login, you are permitted to upload papers, reviewers will decide if your paper can be published on the library. If your account has permission to peer review, you will be asked to peer review from time to time.
What is the peer review procedure?
keyboard_arrow_down
Once your paper has been uploaded to the digital library, the system will automatically select 3 reviewers for it. The reviewers will peer review your paper, then leave comments and decide if your paper can be published. When over 2 of 3 reviewers accepts to publish, the paper will be published and can be found in the library.
How to be a reviewer?
keyboard_arrow_down
In the current period, applying for grants to peer review is not implemented. Reviewers are assigned to specific users by administrators.
What is DL4CSR?
keyboard_arrow_down
DL4CSR is a public blockchain-based digital library service, powered by Hyperledger Fabric open_in_new . The DL4CSR foundation deploys smart contract on peers of organization, and provides APIs for organizations to develop applications. Check DL4CSR API lists open_in_new to get more information.
